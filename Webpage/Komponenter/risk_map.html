Industrial Sector Threats
  • Industrial Espionage: Competitors or nation-states targeting proprietary manufacturing processes
  • Production Sabotage: Manipulation of control systems leading to product defects or equipment damage
  • Safety System Compromise: Disabling critical safety mechanisms causing physical harm
  • Supply Chain Disruption: Attacks against logistics and inventory systems
  • Legacy Equipment: Outdated control systems with no security updates

Notable Attack Vector: Insecure OT/IT boundary integrations

Key Vulnerability: Operational technology not designed with security in mind

Private Home Threats
  • Privacy Invasion: Unauthorized access to cameras, microphones, and personal data
  • Physical Security Breach: Compromised smart locks or security systems
  • Network Penetration: IoT devices as entry points to home networks
  • Botnet Recruitment: Devices enlisted in DDoS attacks
  • Voice Assistant Manipulation: Unauthorized purchases or commands

Notable Attack Vector: Default passwords and weak authentication

Key Vulnerability: Consumer lack of security awareness and configuration knowledge

Public Institution Threats

  • Service Disruption: Attacks affecting public services availability
  • Surveillance System Compromise: Disabled or manipulated security cameras
  • Building Control Systems: HVAC, access control, or emergency systems attacks

Healthcare Threats
  • Patient Safety Risk: Manipulation of life-support or medication delivery systems
  • Medical Data Theft: PHI breaches leading to identity theft or insurance fraud
  • Diagnostic Equipment Tampering: Altered readings leading to misdiagnosis
  • Treatment Disruption: Attacks affecting availability of critical systems
  • Legacy Medical Devices: Outdated equipment with no security updates

Notable Attack Vector: Connected medical devices with inadequate security design

Smart City Threats

  • Mass Surveillance: Compromised city-wide camera networks
