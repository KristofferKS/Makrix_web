HadesGate: IoT Security Firewall

In today's hyper-connected world, IoT devices represent one of the most vulnerable entry points into your network. Traditional security measures often fail to address the unique challenges posed by these devices.

HadesGate is MAKRIX Security's dedicated firewall solution that creates a secure subnet specifically for your IoT devices with specialized firewall rules tailored to their unique requirements and vulnerabilities.

By isolating IoT traffic and implementing granular control over communications, HadesGate ensures that compromised devices cannot be used as gateways to your critical systems and data.

Product Overview

HadesGate is a comprehensive IoT security solution that combines advanced network segmentation with intelligent threat detection to protect your connected devices from emerging threats.

Dedicated IoT Subnet
Creates a separate network segment for all IoT devices, isolating them from critical business systems and sensitive data.
Specialized Firewall Rules
Custom security policies designed specifically for the unique communication patterns and vulnerabilities of IoT devices.
Real-Time Monitoring
ontinuous analysis of IoT traffic patterns with immediate alerts on suspicious activity or potential breach attempts.

Key Benefits

1

Enhanced Security Posture

By isolating IoT devices in their own subnet, HadesGate prevents lateral movement in case of compromise, protecting your critical business assets from IoT-based attacks.

2

Simplified Compliance

Meet regulatory requirements for network segmentation and device isolation with a purpose-built solution that simplifies compliance documentation and reporting.

3

Operational Visibility

Gain comprehensive visibility into all IoT device communications with detailed logging and analytics that reveal usage patterns and potential vulnerabilities.

4

Reduced Attack Surface

Minimize potential entry points by implementing strict control over IoT device communications, only allowing necessary traffic and blocking all unauthorized connection attempts.
